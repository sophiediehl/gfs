Windpipe API

Windpipe data for wind speeds at 80 meters above ground in [meters/second] may be retrieved in CSV format via HTTP requests to specially formatted URLs. You may specify the rectangular region of interest, and choose between a monthly average for a particular month, a daily average for a particular day, or a forecast for the current day.

Data is available from September 2013 through the current day.

To access information via wget in the command line, you would run the command:
wget http://54.193.64.222:5000/api/LAT1/LONG1/LAT2/LONG2/TIMETYPE/YEAR/MONTH/DAY

and replace the fields as follows:

  • LAT1, LONG1: The latitude and longitude, respectively, of southwest corner of the region of interest.
  • LAT2, LONG2: The latitude and longitude, respectively, of the northeast corner of the region of interest.
  • TIMETYPE: M for monthly average, D for daily average, or F for today's forecast.
  • YEAR: the desired year. "na" for today's forecast.
  • MONTH: the desired month. "na" for today's forecast.
  • DAY: the desired day. "na" for today's forecast or monthly average.

For example, to retrieve the monthly average for January, 2014 for all latitudes and longitudes (0.5 degree granularity) between (10.0,10.0) and (20.0, 20.0), run the command:
wget http://54.193.64.222:5000/api/10.0/10.0/20.0/20.0/M/2014/1/na

Data will be returned in the following CSV row format:
latitude, longitude, velocity[m/s] [, hour (UTC timezone) if TIMETYPE=F].
